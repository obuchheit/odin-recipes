Buffalo Chicken Dip

A Perfet Gameday Snack

With a hint of butter and garlic, our creamy Buffalo chicken dip recipe is a stand out hit wherever you serve it. Using rotisserie chicken to beat cooking times, it doesnâ€™t get any easier than this one!

Ingrdeients

  • Frank's Original Hot Sauce
  • Sour Cream
  • Cheddar
  • Blue Cheese
  • Green Onions

Instructions

  1. Soften cream cheese close to room temperature. Take it out of the refrigerator for a good 20-30 before cooking. It will continue to soften once added to the hot skillet.
  2. Shred and pull apart the chicken with two forks or cut into cubes.
  3. Throw all ingredients into a dish and heat until desired
